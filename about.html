Read my story

As a person who graduated from science high school and Political Science and International Relations department of Boğaziçi University, I worked as a tax specialist in international companies for about 3 years. This may seem a bit confusing with considering all of my interests however, I think all of these will have a positive impact on my future career and I am trying to combine my skills.

With starting my career as a Transfer Pricing Consultant, I have prepared many Benchmark Studies which require me to visit many websites of companies in different industries. In this process, I found some of the websites very organized and visually appealing, while others I did not. To learn how to develope a better one, I attended to SheCodes Web Develeopment bootcamps in which I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React and built projects with these skills.

In my career, I also realized that there are too many aspects in Tax and Transfer Pricing which can be automatized with understanding the data initially. Realizing this grew my curiousity toward Data and I attended Big Data and Business Analytics Program in Istanbul Technical University in which I learned about Python, R, Matlab and Knime. Now, I continue my journey with Information Technology Masters Degree in Bahçeşehir University. Coding and learning about technology became my new interests with fun practices.
